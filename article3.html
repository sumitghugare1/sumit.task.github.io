Article 3: "M S Bidve Engineering College Latur"

M.S. Bidve Engineering College (MSBECL), situated in Latur, Maharashtra, India, is a prestigious institution affiliated with Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University and accredited by ISO. Let me share some details about this esteemed college:

  • Establishment: The college was established in 1983 by the Mahatma Basweshwar Education Society (MBES) with the vision of creating engineering graduates equipped with global insights and industry-relevant knowledge.
  • Affiliation: MSBECL is affiliated to Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded.
  • Approval: It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi.
  • Legacy: The institute has celebrated its silver jubilee in 2008 and has a rich legacy of professional excellence in engineering education.
  • Courses: MSBECL offers Bachelor's and Master's programs in various engineering specializations.
  • Alumni: The college boasts a strong network of alumni who have excelled in diverse fields across India and globally.
